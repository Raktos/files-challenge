Jebediah Kerman

Jebediah Kerman the intrepid immortal kerbalnaut takes perilous journeys throughout the solar system. He is a survivor. Though he has experienced so many terrible crashes and failures Jebediah has still not lost his wanderlust, his desire to explore, his curiosity. He will continue to live on the cutting edge of Kerbal's program to the final frontier.

He appears in the game Kerbal Space Program, in which you the player are charged with the managing of planet Kerbal's infant space program.
